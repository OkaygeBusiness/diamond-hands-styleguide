Typography

For typography, we chose Hind and Work Sans to be our main font faces. These are two san-serif fonts that have a simple yet modern and strong design, making it a perfect choice for finance related apps. We also found that the majority of fintech websites use similar or identical font faces, backing up our choices. Having these modern style fonts can also make the overall appearance friendlier for unfamiliar and new users, but also maintaining a professional feel.

For the hierarchy, because Work Sans has a wider letter spacing, we decided to use it for the headings, subheadings, and buttons. Because Hind is slightly taller and has a more narrow letter spacing, we decided to use it for the body text. Also, Hind is less stylized and contains less tails in the letters, making it easier to read (g vs g and a vs a).

Because we want to emphasize readability, we are only going to use font weights semibold, medium, or regular. For font colours, all the text will be black or white, depending on the background. Coloured text, such as green and red for the stock information, or blue for any links will still follow the same hierarchy as the other text.
