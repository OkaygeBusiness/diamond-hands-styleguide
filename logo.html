Logo

Our Diamond Hands logo is inspired by the most popular subreddit r/WallStreetBets (WSB) with over 8 million members where participants discuss stock and option trading. In this subreddit, the term "apes together strong" means that the community is strong and united. "diamond hands" means that the community will hold on to their stocks no matter what. With these phrases in mind, we created our logo.
