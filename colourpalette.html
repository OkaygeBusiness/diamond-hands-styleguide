Colour Palette

For our colour palette, we decided to use greens and blues. Instead of pure white and black, we have gone for lighter colours to help with the welcoming feeling that we want our app to give off. We used green to represent money and wealth for our stock app. Greens are also calmer, which is more friendly to new users. We used blue in our app because of our name, "Diamond Hands," because diamonds usually are represented with the colour blue. Also, we used a light blue in the popular "corporate" applications, so using a similar colour can help give people trust in our app.
