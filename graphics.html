Buttons

Button Sizes

Large: width 9rem (equivalent to 144px)
Medium: width 6rem
Small: width 3rem
XSmall: width 1.5rem


Every container/button will have rounded corners to look friendlier on mobile view. The rounding will be consistent throughout all components.

Icons

All Icons will be monochromatic except for the icons representing a change in stock price. The up arrow will be green and the down arrow will be red, indicating positive or negative.

Icon sizing will be consistent throughout the app. The icons will be 1.5rem (24px) in size.
